The Noble-Loble's Policies

#Warranty Policy

  1. Products receiving warranty are products that do not change skin structure, skin color, ...
  2. The warranty does not apply to products beyond the warranty period, products that are defective due to external forces or after a period of use the sole is worn under physical impact.
  3. Free maintenance 3 times for 1 product within 3 years.
  4. The curing time is from 3 to 5 days depending on the condition of the product that needs maintenance.

#Payment policy

  1. Customers who come to buy shoes directly at the store can pay by cash or swipe card.
  2. Card payments will be free of charge.
  3. Customers who order online can pay directly when receiving the goods
  4. You can pay by Bank Transfer

#Exchange policy

  1. Products are accepted for exchange when there is a manufacturing error
  2. The time limit you can exchange the product is 7 days (from the time of receiving the product).
  3. Customers can exchange products directly at all branches of the Noble - Loble or ship to exchange.
  4. For products exchanged for a lower value, The Noble - oble is non-refundable, only supporting the exchange of excess money for accessories.
